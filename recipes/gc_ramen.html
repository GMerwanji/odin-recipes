Grilled Cheese Ramen

Description

Grilled Cheese. Ramen. Kraft Mac and Cheese? Put this all together and you have one of the greatest inventions of all time.

Cheddar Pork Broth

  • 1 tablespon unsalted butter
  • 1 packet cheese powder from a 6-once box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 1 seasoning paclet from a 3-ounce package of pork-flavored instant ramen
  • 2 cups whole milk

Grilled Cheese

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 3 thin slices deli meat of choice

To finish

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 brick instant ramen noodles
  • 1 scallion, thinly sliced
