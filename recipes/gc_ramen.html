Grilled Cheese Ramen

Description

Grilled Cheese. Ramen. Kraft Mac and Cheese? Put this all together and you have one of the greatest inventions of all time.

Cheddar Pork Broth

  • 1 tablespon unsalted butter
  • 1 packet cheese powder from a 6-once box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 1 seasoning paclet from a 3-ounce package of pork-flavored instant ramen
  • 2 cups whole milk

Grilled Cheese

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 3 thin slices deli meat of choice

To finish

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 brick instant ramen noodles
  • 1 scallion, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Put a medium pot to medium heat
  2. Add the butter. Once melted add the ramen packet and Kraft Mac and Cheese packet. Stir
  3. Once foaming, add a splash of milk. Mix it all together before adding the rest of the milk, along with 1 cup of water
  4. Bring the liquid to a simmer, then let it roll for about 30 seconds
  5. Remove from heat
  6. Place a nonstick or cast-iron skillet over medium-low heat
  7. Add 1 tablespoon of butter and let it melt and get foamy
  8. Lay the bread in the skillet until golden brown then flip over.
  9. Lay two slices of cheese on top of the slices of bread, then the meat, then the remaining cheese.
  10. Place the bread onto the cheese and use the remaining tablespoon of butter to slowly cook (low heat) the bread bottom, about four minutes. Flip over and do this again. Place on a cutting board to rest once done
  11. Bring a pot of water to boil. Crack your egg into a small ramekin, making sure not to break the yolk. Use a spoon to swirl the boiling water in a circle and make a vortex. Tilt the egg into the water, remove from heat, cover the pot, and poach the egg until the white is set and the yolk is runny, about 4 minutes.
  12. Lift out the egg with a slotted spoon and put it on a paper towel to drain.
  13. Bring the water to boil again and cook the ramen noodles. Drain and place in a bowl
  14. Bring the ramen broth back to a simmer, then ladle it over the noodles. Lay the poached egg on one side, then cut the grilled cheese into 4 long batons. Place on ramen and top with scallions
