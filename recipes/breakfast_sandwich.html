California-Style NYC Breakfast Sandwich

Description

New York style breakfast sandwich with a little bit of a California twist. Easy, Delicious, and Portable.

  • 3 or 4 slices of bacon or pancetta
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, plus more for the rolls
  • 2 large eggs
  • Fine sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 small soft rolls, cut in half
  • 2 slices any kind of cheese
  • 1 small or 1/2 large avocado, thinly sliced
  • 2 handfuls of arugula
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Flaky sea salt
